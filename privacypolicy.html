Infinity Garage Door, LLC Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use personal information from users of our website, https://thegaragedoorworks.com/.

We collect information you provide voluntarily, such as name, address, email, and billing information for purchases and service requests.

We also collect information automatically through cookies, server logs, and similar technologies. This may include your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

Information Use

We use your information to provide services, improve our offerings, send promotional emails (with consent), and contact you for surveys or market research (with consent).

Disclosure of Information

We will not sell or rent your information to third parties. We may disclose information in limited circumstances, such as for legal requirements, to service providers, or to protect our rights and property.

We may contact you for non-marketing purposes, such as security breaches, account issues, or changes to our services or privacy policy.

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn such information has been collected, we will delete it.
