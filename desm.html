Garage Door Repair Services for Homeowners in Des Moines, WA

7 days a week, evenings & holidays The Garage Door Works is proud to be the go-to provider for top-notch residential garage door services in Des Moines, Washington. Our steadfast commitment to prioritizing the safety and security of your home is seen in our prompt and professional garage door repair solutions.


The Repairs We Complete

Our team is here to help with broken torsion springs, malfunctioning openers, damaged panels, or off-track doors. Also, our trucks are fully equipped with the necessary tools and parts, as we strive to complete most repairs on the same day. This ensures minimal disruption to your day. We are also open seven days a week, including most major holidays, so your garage door is fixed as soon as possible.


The Garage Door Solutions We Offer

We provide a comprehensive range of garage door services that will help keep your doors in optimal condition. Our specialized "lube and tune" service focuses on lubrication and fine-tuning, enhancing the reliability of your garage door. We also complete a detailed safety inspection as part of our service.


Quality Replacement Options

We source all of our garage doors and openers from industry-leading manufacturers. This includes Amarr, C.H.I., Clopay, and LiftMaster. You can select from a variety of attractive styles, colors, and decorative options to customize your doors and match your home's curb appeal. We also offer garage doors that are built with advanced technology for security and functionality.


Customer-Centric Approach

At The Garage Door Works, are a licensed, bonded, and insured company. We can address all of your garage door repair and maintenance needs. We also offer complimentary estimates, conduct 26-point garage door safety inspections on every job, and offer transparent recommendations.
