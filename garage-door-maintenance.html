Homeowners often overlook the upkeep of their garage doors, yet it plays an integral role in a home's functionality and safety. The Garage Door Works, in our time serving the Seattle, Washington region, has seen firsthand how important proper maintenance is and has made it our mission to educate customers. Garage Door Solution

Comprehensive Maintenance Services with Our 26-Point Inspection

At The Garage Door Works, we provide a broad range of maintenance services designed to keep your garage door running smoothly. Our signature “lube and tune” service, which includes lubrication and tune-up of all parts, balancing the door, and adjusting settings, will extend the longevity of key garage door components. We also provide a detailed 26-point garage door safety inspection, which ensures all components are functioning correctly and no potential problems are overlooked. If something is wrong with your garage door, we'll catch it now before it causes a dent in your bank account.

Choosing us for your garage door maintenance means you are investing in quality. From inspecting hardware for wear and tear, lubricating moving parts, to adjusting spring tension, we handle it all.
